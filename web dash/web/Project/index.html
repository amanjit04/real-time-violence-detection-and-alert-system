The Pseudo Byte

Image Enhancement

Image enhancement refers to the process of improving the visual quality of an image, typically by applying various modifications to its appearance.

Frame Generation

Video framing refers to the process of positioning and composing the objects, people, and scenery within the video frame..

Object Detection

Object detection is a computer vision technique that involves locating and classifying objects within an image or video.

Weapon Detection

Weapon detection is a subset of object detection that specifically focuses on detecting the presence of weapons in images or video footage.

Motion Detection

Motion detection is a technique used in computer vision and video surveillance to detect the presence of movement in a video sequence.

Facial Recognition

Facial recognition is a biometric technology that uses computer algorithms to identify and verify the identity of a person based on their facial features.

Alert Generation

Alert generation refers to the process of automatically generating alerts or notifications based on certain predefined criteria.
